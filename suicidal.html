Suicidal Feelings

Having a suicidal feeling is when you feel the urge to end your life.  

Causes of suicidal feelings 

There are many causes of stress and they include: 

  • Alcohol and drug abuse
  • Depression
  • Social isolation and loneliness
  • Terminal illness or chronic pain
  • Recent loss or stressful life event.
  • History of trauma or abuse.
  • Major life changes, such as loss of independence.
  • Exposure to other teen suicides.
  • Hostile social or school environment.
  • Loss of sense of purpose.

What can I do to suicidal feelings

You can do the following to help you manage suicidal feelings:

  • Learn and read more about stress
  • Stay away from drugs and alcohol
  • Replace anxious thoughts with positive ones
  • Learn how to relax
  • Eat healthily and exercise
  • Talk to a trusted friend, adult, or family 
  • Seek help from a professional counselor 
  • Do something fun or nice for yourself
